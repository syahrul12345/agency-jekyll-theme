About

General Standard will be Singapore's first cryptocurrency gateway which is simple and easy to use with a guaranteed 100% uptime.

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    General Standard was conceptualized in 2015, however it was merely a side thought of the founder, Mr Syahrul Nizam who was an early cryptocurrency advocate

  • June 2016

    Creation of the team

    New members were added to the team, however this was merely a side-project for many of the team members.

  • January 2017

    Earlier Service

    Officially started as a small "friends only" exchange, Syahrul Nizam sold bitcoins and ethereum to close friends who were interested in getting started. There was no web interface, and sales were made peer to peer.

  • July 2017

    Online transactions

    In contact with several online payment processors to allow people to purchase cryptocurrencies without meeting up in person.

  • August 2017

    Halt of online transactions

    High fees demande by online payment processors such as paypal and payoneer made Syahrul Nizam lose money selling cryptoccurency. In addition, because of the high volume he was facing, his account was constantly under investigation and subsequently closed down due to 'high risk'. Local Banks were also not supportive of cryptocurrencies for obvious reasons.

  • 30 August 2017

    General Standard

    General Standard Exchange Platform was conceptualized. The platforms aims to provide a simple solution for the masses to buy cryptocurrencies. In addition, General Standard, will be fully regulated by the Government and in compliance with terrorism laws. We will be working with Visa and Mastercard,so that we can process credit card payments directly.

  • September 2017

    Singapore Digital Dollar Token Conceptualized

    The Singapore Digital Dollar(SDD) will be used as 'fuel' to purchase crytpocurrency on the General Standard platform. SDD aims to tokenize the Singapore Dollar as well as provide funds to the core team. Every trade done on the General Standard platform will use the SDD token
